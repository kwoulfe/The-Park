ABOUT

Notorious for their ability to bring raw honest groove and captivating energy to whatever musical context they find themselves in, The Park’s musical diversity and astounding musicianship has allowed them to cover much ground. With Derek Taylor, Josh Lippi, Ben Schwier and Nate Mercereau making up the core of the group, The Park’s playground has grown to include some of the most talented and sought after musicians in the industry.

Aside from there own releases The Process..., These Are The Days, and their collaboration with Soul Legend Darondo on Silver Cloud Time Machine, The Park’s musical range and diversity can be heard on a number of album releases supporting artists such as Alice Russell, Grillade, Jesse Boykins III, Big K.R.I.T., Nino Moschella, Anthony Valadez, Chuck Prophet, Mara Hruby, BANKS, K.Flay, Rhye, Logic, Lee Bob & the Truth, Quasi Bonsai, Josh Lippi & The Overtimers, Tommy Guerrero, and many more.
